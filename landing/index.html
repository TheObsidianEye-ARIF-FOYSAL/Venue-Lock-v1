How it works

From empty hall to checked-in crowd

Four steps, one app, three kinds of user — and a headcount that can't drift.

1

Lay out the venue

Create sections as rows × columns. Publishing fixes the capacity and mints a six-character access code.

2

Share the code

Attendees enter it to see the live seat map. Volunteers use the same code to apply to work the door.

3

They pick a seat

One tap claims an exact seat and generates a QR entry pass, stored on their phone.

4

Scan them in

Point the camera at the pass. Counts update live on every device at the venue.

Features

Built for the moment doors open

Everything an event needs to fill every seat and not one more.

Seat-level booking

Attendees pick an exact seat from a live map — capacity enforced on the server, never oversold.

QR entry passes

Every booking generates a scannable pass, saved on-device — survives a force-close, no reprinting.

Live camera check-in

Admins and volunteers scan passes at the door with the phone's own camera — instant validation.

Reserve for guests

Block off seats for VIPs or staff before doors open — public booking simply skips them.

Volunteer teams

Approve door staff per venue with a device-scoped token — no shared admin logins at the gate.

Real-time sync

Seat status refreshes across every device at the venue — one booking updates everyone.

Roles

One app, three roles

Everyone at the event gets exactly the view they need.

🛡️ Admin

Create venues, configure seat maps, reserve seats, review volunteers, scan at the door.

🎫 Audience

Join with a venue code, pick a seat, get an entry pass that stays on the device.

📷 Volunteer

Apply with a venue's access code, get approved, and scan passes with a scoped device token.
