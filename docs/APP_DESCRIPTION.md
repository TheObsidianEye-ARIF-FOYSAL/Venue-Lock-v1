## Full description (store listing body)

VenueLock is a seat-exact event check-in system for organizers who need to
know — for certain — that a venue never gets oversold.

**How it works**

1. An admin creates a venue and lays out its seat map, sets a join code, and
   shares it with attendees.
2. Attendees open VenueLock, enter the join code, and pick an exact open
   seat from a live seat map. Booking a seat that's already taken is
   impossible — capacity is enforced on the server, not just in the UI.
3. Every booking generates a QR entry pass, saved on the attendee's device
   so it survives a force-close or restart — no reprinting, no "I lost my
   ticket."
4. At the door, admins or approved volunteers scan each pass with the
   phone's own camera for instant, live check-in.

**Built for three roles**

- **Admin** — create and configure venues, reserve seats for VIPs or staff
  before doors open, review and approve volunteer applications, scan at the
  door.
- **Audience** — join with a venue code, book a seat, carry the entry pass
  on-device.
- **Volunteer** — apply to a specific venue with its access code, get
  approved by the admin, and scan passes with a device-scoped token — no
  shared admin logins floating around at the gate.

**Why organizers use it**

- No overbooking: seat capacity is enforced server-side, not by trust.
- No paper: QR passes live on the attendee's phone.
- No bottleneck at the door: multiple volunteers can scan in parallel, each
  with their own scoped access.
- No spreadsheet reconciliation: seat status updates live across every
  device at the venue.
